Hello everyone!

My name Is Svetlana Lebedeva. I am a UC Berkeley senior, passionate about marketing, design and technology entrepreneurship. I have always strived for prospects of building my career around these exciting fields, while simultaneously pursuing the higher goal of benefiting my local community and beyond.

After almost a decade of living in the United States and working for a few start-ups, I have acquired a level of competence sufficient to attempt my own entrepreneurial endeavor. Last year, together with my fellow team-members, I cofounded Lucky Spot, a platform for sharing residential parking.

Founding Lucky Spot taught me that startup life is not as glamorous as it may seem to an outsider – it requires a lot of enthusiasm, dedication and hard work. Through this challenging, yet rewarding experience I have learned about the importance of proficiency with technology, necessity of establishing the right network and the complexities involved in acquiring venture capital funding.

Being a first generation transfer student from a foreign country, I feel forever grateful to be a part of such a great school as UC Berkeley and be tough by brilliant professors such as Jon Burgstone, Ken Singer, Robert Chandra, Reza Moazammi and many more!

Thank you for visiting and hope to hear from you!
